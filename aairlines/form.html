In the case of a client requesting a cancellation of a booked service, provided that the booking is eligible for cancellation according to the regulations of the airline, Alternative Airlines will waive the administration fee of $60.00 per person, which is typically applied in the Standard Service Package. In the case of a client requesting booking alterations (e.g. change of date, re-routing) on the booked service, provided that the booking is eligible to such amendments according to the regulations of the airline, Alternative Airlines will waive the administration fee of $60.00 per person, which is typically applied in Standard Service Package. The waiver of the administration fee is exclusively available to customers who have purchased the premium service package, which may only be purchased at the time of the ticket purchase and is non-transferable to other bookings.
