Create your own Tournament

Easily create and organize your own golf tournament within just 3 steps now!

Apple Store Google Play

Our organizer features gives you the ability to cast a wider net of golf courses, dates, and reach of players who can participate in your tournament.

ActionGolfers can have an assigned account manager help you setup your tournament and customize a dedicated landing page if needed.

As an organizer you’ll be able to upload a logo along with a description of your company or charity. This can also include the purpose of the tournament or destination of any proceeds received.

Participants that play at those designated courses during your specific dates will see your tournament and have an opportunity to join your game at zero cost to the fundraiser.

Step 1.

Easily create a tournament within the few minutes:

  • Click into your My Game.
  • Select Private Tournament.

Step 2.

Start adding details for your game:

  • Enter in a unique name for your tournament and select the range of dates.
  • Select the golf course you’d like to use, how many holes, and from what color tees you’ll be starting at.
  • Decide entry fees & the distribution of funds or prizes for winners.
  • Finally, how many players will be allowed to play and start inviting people automatically to your game!

Step 3.

Final steps to launching your tournament:

  • Verify all your information is correct before pressing confirm.
  • Once launched, you’ll have a live view of current players in your tournament and their scores on the leaderboard!
  • When the tournament is concluded you’ll be able to withdrawal the funds for distribution or send prizes to winners!
